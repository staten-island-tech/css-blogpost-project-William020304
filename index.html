Mooncakes

What are Mooncakes?

Mooncakes are a traditional Chinese Bakery product that are eaten during the Mid-Autumn Festival. The crust are made out of lard and vegetable oil and they are filled with red bean or egg custards Click For More!

In Chinese cultures, Mooncakes resemble family, love, good luck, and best wishes. The circular shape of the moon cake symbolizes the moon. The imprint on the top of the mooncake means "harmony" in Chinese. Click For More!

My favorite type of Mooncake is the Cantonese style Mooncake. Although there are many variation of the Cantonese Mooncake, my favorite has at least one salted egg yolk covered with lotus seed paste. Click For More!
